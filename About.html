A little introduction...

I’m a Pharmaceutical Process Development Specialist with over 7 years of experience in optimizing manufacturing workflows, scaling up production systems, and ensuring regulatory compliance in highly controlled environments.

My expertise bridges the gap between laboratory innovation and commercial-scale manufacturing, with a focus on:

  • Designing robust, efficient processes for drug development and large-scale production
  • Implementing quality-by-design (QbD) principles to meet FDA and EMA standards
  • Driving cost-effective technology transfers from R&D to GMP manufacturing
  • Leading cross-functional teams to deliver safe, effective therapies on time

What drives Me

I believe that manufacturing science is the heartbeat of modern medicine. A breakthrough in the lab only reaches patients if it can be manufactured at scale, consistently, and safely. My passion is taking that science and transforming it into reality.

Beyond the lab

  • Exploring advances in biologics and cell therapy manufacturing
  • Mentoring junior engineers and scientists
